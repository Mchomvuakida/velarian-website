Our Story

From Tanzania to the World

Velarian was born from a dream deferred but not defeated. Our founder, Akida, a Tanzanian student in Poland, dreamed of flying as a pilot. When financial barriers stood in the way, he turned to technology and entrepreneurship.

But his journey was marked by struggle: a family without a house of their own, yet sacrificing everything for his education. Witnessing countless others facing even greater challenges, Akida founded Velarian to create pathways where none existed.

Today, Velarian is a movement to turn systemic barriers into launchpads. We started by helping students study abroad affordably, and now we're building a legacy that spans education, technology, housing, and more — all rooted in Africa and built for the world.

Our mission: Unlock potential and opportunity for all, from Africa outward.
